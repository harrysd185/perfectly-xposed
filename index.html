About

Behind the lens

I photograph the moments most people walk past.

I'm a portrait and landscape photographer based in the Pacific Northwest, working with couples, families, and brands who want images that feel like themselves rather than a pose. My approach is unhurried and natural-light led.

Over the past decade I've shot on six continents, from studio sessions to remote alpine mornings — always chasing the same thing: honest light and a little bit of stillness.
